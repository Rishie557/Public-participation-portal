Tax & Finance Reform
Tax Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2024
National Assembly · Passed Nov 2024 · Signed into law 11 Dec 2024 · Effective 27 Dec 2024
Passed
Amends Income Tax Act, VAT Act, Excise Duty Act & Miscellaneous Fees and Levies Act · Introduced Significant Economic Presence Tax (SEPT) replacing Digital Service Tax · Introduced minimum top-up tax of 15% for multinationals · SHIF and housing levy contributions made tax-deductible Source: Bowmans ↗

Passed
